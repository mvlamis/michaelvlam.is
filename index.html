My values

Connection

Staying connected in the 21st century is now more important than ever. Human connection is much more complicated than just texting. Putting two brains together needs communication and a strong bond to be done right.

Play

I don't take myself too seriously. Humans aren't made to stick to the same thing all the time. I don't just allow change, I welcome and embrace it.

Trust

Good people attract good people. I believe in human strength, and that as people we can all accomplish more than we think. I trust in my peers and the people I get to know.
